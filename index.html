About Me

I manage a diversified portfolio with a wide range of instrument, from holding currencies and equities for multiple years to intraday trading on index futures holding positions for as short as a few minutes. Having been in the industry for over 10 years and experienced all kinds of market conditions. the two most important aspect of survival are: Don’t over trade & Don’t over leverage your account. In the heart of trading is identifying the market conditions: uptrend; downtrend; range bound market; choppy markets. However there are thousands of different securities to choose from and multiple time frames in which to trade. The key for a trader is to develop a system which he is compatible from a technical and psychological point of view.
